## 1. Yahoo Finance (`yfinance` API)

**Dashboard Layer:** *High-Frequency Sentiment, Systematic Liquidity, and Risk Architecture.*
*Ingested daily (end-of-day) or via real-time websockets.*

### Sovereign Debt & Risk Infrastructure (The Gilt Matrix)

* **Yield Curve Points:**
* `^GB2YT=F` (2-Year Gilt Future / Yield proxy)
* `^GB5YT=F` (5-Year Gilt Future)
* `^GB10YT=F` (10-Year Gilt Future)
* `^GB30YT=F` (30-Year Gilt Future)


* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **The 2s/10s Yield Spread:** The primary leading recession indicator.
* **The 10s/30s Yield Spread:** Measures long-term structural liability matching and inflation hedging stress.



### Currency Dynamics & Imported Inflation Channels

* **Spot FX Pairs:** `GBPUSD=X` (Cable), `GBPEUR=X`, `GBPJPY=X`, `GBPCNY=X`.
* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **Sterling Real-Time Momentum:** Roll-up index tracking trade-weighted currency strength against a basket of main trade partners.



### Equity Market Spreads (Corporate Health & Concentration)

* **Headline Indices:** `^FTSE` (FTSE 100), `^FTMC` (FTSE 250), `^FTAI` (FTSE AIM All-Share).
* **Sector-Specific Proxies:**
* `CNA.L` (Centrica) / `BP.L` (BP) / `SHEL.L` (Shell) — Energy & utility cost exposure.
* `BARC.L` (Barclays) / `LLOY.L` (Lloyds) / `NWG.L` (NatWest) — Domestic banking credit risk and net interest margin proxies.
* `TW.L` (Taylor Wimpey) / `BDEV.L` (Barratt Developments) — Real-time housebuilder equity sentiment (collated as a sector basket).


* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **FTSE 250 / FTSE 100 Ratio:** Direct gauge of purely domestic UK economic performance against global, multinational corporate revenue.



### Fixed Income Credit & Systemic Fear

* **Corporate Debt Risk:** `SLXX.L` (iShares £ Corporate Bond ETF), `IHYG.L` (iShares £ High Yield Corporate Bond ETF).
* **Volatility Proxy:** Tracking trailing standard deviation, intraday trading ranges, and volume spikes on front-month FTSE index futures.
* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **UK Corporate Credit Spread:** `Yield of SLXX.L` minus `^GB10YT=F`. A widening gap signals rising corporate default risks and tightening bank credit.

## 2. HM Land Registry (HMLR Open Data & Bulk Datasets)

**Dashboard Layer:** *Structural Wealth Assets, Private Sector Credit Creation, and Institutional Capital Flight.*
*Ingested monthly via HMLR Linked Data API, bulk .csv downloads, or turtle (.ttl) triple stores.*

### The UK House Price Index (UK HPI Dataset)

* **Geographic Granular Data:** Average price, monthly change (%), annual change (%), and index score broken down hierarchically: *UK → Country (England/Wales) → Region (e.g., Greater London) → Local Authority (e.g., Wandsworth).*
* **Property Type Split:** Dedicated indexing for Detached, Semi-Detached, Terraced, and Flats/Maisonettes.
* **Funding Split:** Index paths separated by **Cash Buyers vs. Mortgage Buyers**.
* **Buyer Status Split:** Index paths separated by **First-Time Buyers vs. Former Owner-Occupiers**.

### Raw Price Paid Data (PPD - Transaction Level Logging)

* **Data Fields per Transaction:** Unique Transaction Identifier, Price Paid (£), Date of Transfer, Postcode, Property Type, New Build Flag (`Y/N`), Tenure Type (`Freehold/Leasehold`), and Transaction Category (`A`: Standard market sales, `B`: Repossessions, buy-to-lets, and corporate transfers).
* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **Transaction Velocity Indicator:** Rolling 3-month volume count by region. Drops in volume are a strict leading indicator of price turning points.
* **New-Build Premium Tracker:** Spreads between `New Build = Y` vs `New Build = N` over time to spot localized developer oversupply.



### Institutional, Commercial & Foreign Capital Movements

* **Commercial Real Estate (CRE) Activity:** Transaction volumes and price trends parsed from non-residential registrations.
* **Overseas Companies Corporate Ownership Dataset:** Total count, country of origin, and property value of land holdings held by non-UK corporate entities.
* **UK Companies Property Ownership Dataset:** Properties held by domestic corporate entities (tracking institutional build-to-rent and corporate portfolio growth).
* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **Foreign Capital Flight Metric:** Net monthly change in properties held by offshore entities (e.g., British Virgin Islands, Jersey corporations holding London real estate).



### Title Application and Legal Charge Volume (Credit Velocity)

* **Transaction Data Dataset:** Monthly volumes of all administrative applications processed: *Transactions for Value (Sales)* vs. *Discharges of Charges (Mortgages paid off)* vs. *New Legal Charges (New Mortgages/Liens registered)*.
* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **Net Credit Creation Velocity:** `New Legal Charges` minus `Discharges`. A negative net figure reveals systemic private-sector deleveraging.

## 3. HM Treasury (HMT / Debt Management Office)

**Dashboard Layer:** *Fiscal Policy Anchors, Sovereign Balance Sheets, and Institutional Baseline Targets.*
*Ingested monthly or dynamically post-Budget/Statement events via official publications, DMO tables, and national statistical releases.*

### The Consensus Economics Framework

* **Independent Forecast Compendium:** Monthly updated table of compiled forecasts from up to 40 City banks and research institutes covering:
* GDP Growth (Current Year and Next Year)
* CPI & RPI Inflation rates
* Claimant Count & LFS Unemployment rates
* Public Sector Net Borrowing (PSNB)
* Current Account Balance


* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **Macro Standard Deviation:** Gauging consensus tight-alignment vs high-divergence (a high variance between bank forecasts acts as an early proxy for systemic economic uncertainty).



### Public Sector Financial Soundness & Debt Management

* **Fiscal Balance Aggregates:** Public Sector Net Borrowing (PSNB), Public Sector Net Debt (PSND), and Public Sector Net Debt as a % of GDP.
* **Liquidity Demands:** Central Government Net Cash Requirement (CGNCR).
* **HMT / DMO Gilt Issuance Remit:** Annual issuance target broken down explicitly by:
* Short Conventional Gilts (Planned gross billions)
* Medium Conventional Gilts
* Long Conventional Gilts
* Index-Linked Gilts (Inflation-protected)
* Green Gilt Issuance targets


* **Calculated Dashboard Derivatives:**
* **Sovereign Issuance Absorption Ratio:** Trailing coverage ratios from DMO auctions to evaluate international institutional demand for UK debt.



### Policy Thresholds & Macroeconomic Backstops

* **Fiscal Drag Parameters:** Official tax boundaries for Personal Allowance, Higher Rate Threshold, National Insurance bands, and Corporation Tax rates.
* **Public Expenditure Statistical Analyses (PESA):** Departmental Outturn data mapping out precisely how much capital is actively exiting Treasury accounts into the real economy (NHS, Infrastructure, Defence) vs. allocated budgets.
* **Contingent Liabilities Log:** Annual auditing of state-backed corporate loan guarantees, insurance schemes, and public-private partnership liabilities.
